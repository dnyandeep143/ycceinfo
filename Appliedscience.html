Applied Science Department
With an aim to broaden the educational background of students, the department offers studies in applied physics, applied chemistry, applied mathematics and humanities. The department of applied physics has a well-equipped LASER laboratory and has made significant research contribution on International level in the fields of acoustics and solid state physics. Offering facilities of testing and consultancy, the department of applied chemistry, focuses its research on biodiesel, synthesis of natural products, waste water treatment, adsorption etc. Basic research activities by the department of mathematics include providing necessary theory, numerical and computational techniques. The department of humanities provides studies in economics as well as psychological and sociological subjects.
